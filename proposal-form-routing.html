01 How a submission travels

A visitor picks the option that fits them, fills in a short set of fields, and hits send. Every submission follows the same path — only the destination inbox changes based on what they picked.

STEP 1
Visitor picks a request type
Five options on the form. Their choice sets the routing.
STEP 2
Routed to the right inbox
Sent only to the team responsible for that request type.
STEP 3
Visitor gets a confirmation
An automatic reply confirms you received it, within seconds.
STEP 4
A person replies
Your team responds within one business day.

03 Applies to every submission

Sender confirmation
The person who submitted gets an automatic “we received your request” email with a reference number, sent from notifications@tidewaterproperty.com.
Reply-to address
Replies to any automated email go to info@tidewaterproperty.com.
Always copied
admin@alloygp.co (Alloy GP) is cc'd on every submission so we can confirm delivery and catch any issues.
If a category has no inbox
It falls back to the default: info@tidewaterproperty.com + admin@alloygp.co. (Today, only “Submit a bid” uses this fallback.)
Spam & failures
Bot submissions are silently discarded. If an email ever fails to send, our team is alerted instantly so nothing is lost.
