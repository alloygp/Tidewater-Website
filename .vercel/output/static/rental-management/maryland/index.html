Maryland Compliance

The statutes that govern Maryland rentals.

Maryland is among the more rigorous Mid-Atlantic states on landlord-tenant law. Owners outside the state are often surprised. We track every requirement — you don’t have to.

Md. Code · Real Property § 8

Maryland Landlord-Tenant Law

Maryland's landlord-tenant framework: security deposits (max 1 month's rent + interest accrual), required disclosures, repair-and-deduct, eviction notice periods. We operate inside it by default.

Compliance handled, not delegated
County-level

County Rental Licensing

Anne Arundel, Baltimore, Montgomery, Prince George's, and Howard each require separate rental licenses with their own inspection cadence, fees, and renewal schedules. We file, track, and renew on your behalf.

No license lapses, ever
Federal &amp; state

Lead-Paint Compliance

Pre-1978 Maryland rentals require lead-paint inspection & tenant disclosure. We coordinate inspections, file the necessary certificates, and ensure every lease includes the required EPA disclosure.

Critical for pre-1978 properties
