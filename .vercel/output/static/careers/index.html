Why work here

A real career, not a regional account.

If you’ve worked in property management, you know the difference between firms that invest in their people and firms that don’t. Here’s what investment looks like at Tidewater.

01

Credentials, paid upfront

CMCA, AMS, PCAM, SHRM, CPA, real estate licensing, we cover the cost of every CAI designation for managers, administrators, and accountants. Career growth without a tuition bill.

02

Open-door ownership

Stanley and Marc are in the building. Department lunches, summer BBQs, annual events, daily motivation sessions. Family ownership isn't in the brochure — it's the calendar invite.

03

In-house everything

Accounting, HR, QA, marketing, vendor compliance, maintenance, brokerage, technology. Nothing outsourced — which means cross-team problems get solved across the hallway.

04

Built on real tooling

CINC Systems, AppFolio, Microsoft Copilot at every desk, a proprietary in-house vendor compliance app, SharePoint workflow library. We invest in the tools so the work shows up faster.

05

Career runway

Many of our directors started as community managers. The Senior Director of Operations has been here 20+ years. Tenure isn't the exception — it's the pattern.

06

A book of business worth being part of

400+ communities. 40,000+ homes. Six states. The largest privately-owned firm in the region — and one that's been chosen, not acquired.
