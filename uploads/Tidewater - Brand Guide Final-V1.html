01 — Brand Overview
Who We Are

Tidewater Companies is a family-owned, full-service real estate management group serving the Mid-Atlantic since 1989. Three companies under one roof — property management, maintenance, and realty — united by a commitment to hands-on service and forward progress.

Founded

1989 — Over 35 years of continuous, family-owned operation in the Maryland/DC region. Still independently owned by Stanley Greenberg (CEO/Founder) and Marc Greenberg (President).

What We Do

HOA and community association management, rental property management, property maintenance, and real estate sales across Maryland, DC, Virginia, Pennsylvania, Delaware, and West Virginia.

Who We Serve

02 — Logo Usage
Our Mark

The Tidewater logo features the distinctive teal brushstroke circle with the company name in a clean, rounded sans-serif. Two primary versions exist for different background contexts.

White version — for dark backgrounds
Dark version — for light backgrounds
White version on Tidewater Teal
Dark version on White Cream

Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the white logo on dark backgrounds (Dark Tide, Tidewater Teal)
  • Use the dark logo on light backgrounds (White Cream, Soft Linen, Morning Mist)
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "t" character on all sides
  • Use provided logo files only — do not recreate
  • Ensure the teal brushstroke circle remains visible and unobstructed

Don't

  • Place the dark logo on dark backgrounds or the white logo on light backgrounds
  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or distort the logo in any way
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
  • Change the colors of the logo text or brushstroke
  • Place the logo on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds without sufficient contrast

05 — Voice & Tone
How We Sound

Tidewater's voice is warm, grounded, and reassuring with a practical edge. We communicate from expertise without talking down to clients. Professional but approachable — never corporate.

Warm Grounded Reassuring Practical Confident Approachable
The trusted neighbor who also happens to be the most experienced property professional in the region — hands-on, personally invested, and steady enough that you stop worrying about what you've handed off.
